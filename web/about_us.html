About Us

At Ramsay's restaurant, our passion is to connect our guests to the very best of our destinations.Our restaurant offer guests extraordinary places, created by combining unique architecture and structure, expressive decor and artistry, and magnificent features. Add great service, and the result is an unforgettable guest experience. We’re proud to welcome you to our collection. We invite you to think of these, as your home base for all of your adventures. Our restaurant was among the first buildings to be erected in Colombo, we can boast that our communities literally grew up around us. Today, we have added many modern city-center properties to our collection, that allow you to join the bustle of the city and enjoy the culture, flavors and sounds of local life. And, if urban excitement isn’t on your itinerary, our restaurant welcome you to some of the most breathtaking , where you can bask on a safari, challenge yourself on a stunning camping and feel the rush of fresh powder on a BBQ grill. While we aim to give you an authentic experience of the locale whenever you stay with us, we also guarantee consistent high standards throughout our restaurant. Contemporary amenities and timeless elegance — inspired by our unique restaurant — are reflected throughout every service. Make RAMSAY'S your personal gateway to exploring the world’s most extraordinary places—and to having your most memorable experiences.
